ISTEA is a political movement that has developed of late that is trying to enable municipalities to do something with the money that comes from the gas tax other than building roads with it.

See, them highway lobby folks ain't stupid. They saw a gas tax coming way before it came, and they said, you gonna tax the shit, you gonna give us that money right back.

And we have been. Gas tax money has always been funneled into a fund to be used exclusively for road and highway development. So you buy the car, you buy the gas, you buy the roads. And everybody be happy.

Well, not quite.

 
  armchair #0005 - Friday, October 4, 1996
