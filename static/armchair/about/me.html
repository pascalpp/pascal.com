about the dude in the armchair

my name is pascal. i am 25 going on 47. i am having a hard time with this life, being a cat was much easier. i don't believe in reincarnation. i spent much of my childhood in a house on carnation street in mobile, alabama. my keyboard is grungy and stiff. i like to play in the stream of consciousness. damn, i am one cheesy muthafucka.

i moved to wisconsin, maybe that's my problem. i am happy here. i can sometimes close my eyes and think there are no cars. that was impossible in mobile, difficult even here. i hate cars. i don't eat meat. i'm presently all fucked up about sex and intimacy, so i am focusing on my friendships, which have historically been more stable. shit, this is not about me, this is so temporal. oh well, fuck it. i'll change it later, the next time i change.

what else about me? i'm a radical wacky guy. actually i'm pretty boring and straight. or somewhere in between. it's a rainbow of fruit flavors, dig? you got to point the finger for yourself, i can't do it for ya. best way to find out about me is to keep on reading, i guess. or come here!


armchair - january 12, 1996
